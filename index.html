In pursuit of computational photography

Learn what your camera can do

The standard and computational photography features in OM SYSTEM cameras are powerful but can be hard to figure out from a 500-page manual. OlyProbe is a free, simple application for Olympus and OM SYSTEM photographers that collects and reports all the available camera controls and their acceptable values for most newer models.

What OlyProbe does

01

Complete capabilities map

Every camera has different features. OlyProbe finds out what each camera is capable of and produces a capabilities map that reveals all the controls available on each camera model.

02

Cross-camera analysis

OlyProbe gives camera shoppers and multi-camera shooters an easy way to compare and contrast the capabilities of different camera models.

03

Setting cheats

In addition to capturing all the controls and allowable settings, OlyProbe also records all the current settings at probe time, creating a cheat sheet for easy recall and setup for later shoots.

Coming soon from Computography Lab

OlyComp — creative remote control

Now under development, OlyComp is a remote-control app for setting all standard controls and functions and also creating computational control sequences that are not available in the camera. These include:

  • Bulb-ramped and variable speed time-lapse
  • Scripted asymmetrical exposure bracketing
  • Multi-setting memory for infinite custom settings
  • Live preview with real-time setting adjustment
